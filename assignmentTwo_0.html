Week Two: CNC Routing

I was initially trained on the shaper by Madison, first making a design in Adobe Illustrator, then filling it with the shaper-appropriate colors, going through the safety training, learning about the different shaper bits, taping down my board, following instructions to carve out a coaster, and then sanding down the coaster's rough edges at the end.

I then followed Madison's Youtube videos to create my design in Fusion 360 and Adobe Illustrator. Missing are photos of measuring my objects (two drawing utensils and a mug) with a measuring tape. This is a link to my files.

I then imported the .svg file, and got to work on the shaper!

After finishing up my shaper work, I sanded down the organizer and fit in the objects.
